Cape Town
Good to Know
Personal Safety
Keep bags in front of you on busy streets. Don't leave anything visible in a car. Use Uber after dark rather than walking alone. Just a few habits worth having.
Weather
Pack a layer even in summer — the southeaster picks up in the afternoon. Check the forecast before outdoor plans.
Table Mountain
Cable car closes in cloud and wind, sometimes without warning. Check conditions before making the trip.
Check Conditions
Load Shedding
SA has scheduled power outages. The building has backup power for lifts and common areas. Solar lights in cupboard. Check your schedule on EskomSePush.
EskomSePush App
Water & Electricity
Tap water is safe and excellent. Type M plugs — three large round pins, 230V. Spare adaptor in the iron cupboard.
Tipping
10–15% at restaurants. R5–R20 for parking attendants. Always appreciated.
